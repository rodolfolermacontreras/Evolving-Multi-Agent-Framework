Sprint-level details live in `sprints/PI-{N}/CURRENT_PI.md` once sprints begin. Tech debt
details live in backlog items.

## Symbolic Cadence Note

PIs and Sprints are **symbolic units** in this framework, not wall-clock periods. With an
AI fleet, calendar-time per increment compresses dramatically. Cadence exists to provide
ceremony rhythm (planning, retro, demo) -- not to gate calendar weeks.
